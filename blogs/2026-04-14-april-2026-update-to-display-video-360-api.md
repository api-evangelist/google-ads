<p>
Today we’re announcing the April 2026 update to the Display & Video 360 API. This update includes the following:
</p><ul>

<li>Support for additional <code><a href="https://developers.google.com/display-video/api/reference/rest/v4/Exchange?utm_source=googleblog-adsdevs&amp;utm_medium=blog&amp;utm_campaign=adr-dv360api-update-apr2026&amp;utm_content=learnmore-refdocs" target="_blank">Exchange</a></code> and <code><a href="https://developers.google.com/display-video/api/reference/rest/v4/advertisers.lineItems#ThirdPartyVendor?utm_source=googleblog-adsdevs&amp;utm_medium=blog&amp;utm_campaign=adr-dv360api-update-apr2026&amp;utm_content=learnmore-refdocs" target="_blank">ThirdPartyVendor</a></code> values.
<li><code><a href="https://developers.google.com/display-video/api/reference/rest/v4/advertisers.adAssets#AdAsset?utm_source=googleblog-adsdevs&amp;utm_medium=blog&amp;utm_campaign=adr-dv360api-update-apr2026&amp;utm_content=learnmore-refdocs" target="_blank">AdAsset</a></code> identifier fields in objects used in <code><a href="https://developers.google.com/display-video/api/reference/rest/v4/advertisers.adGroupAds#AdGroupAd?utm_source=googleblog-adsdevs&amp;utm_medium=blog&amp;utm_campaign=adr-dv360api-update-apr2026&amp;utm_content=learnmore-refdocs" target="_blank">AdGroupAd</a></code> resources.
<li>Beta support for <a href="https://support.google.com/displayvideo/answer/15598748?utm_source=googleblog-adsdevs&amp;utm_medium=blog&amp;utm_campaign=adr-dv360api-update-apr2026&amp;utm_content=learnmore-dv-hc" target="_blank">Demand Gen</a> resource management.</li></ul>

<p>
<strong>While Demand Gen campaign support is in beta, it is only available to allowlisted customers. </strong>See our <a href="https://developers.google.com/display-video/api/guides/tasks/serve-demand-gen-ad/overview?utm_source=googleblog-adsdevs&amp;utm_medium=blog&amp;utm_campaign=adr-dv360api-update-apr2026&amp;utm_content=learnmore-guide" target="_blank">guide</a> for detailed instructions on how to create a Demand Gen ad campaign.<strong> </strong>If your partner is allowlisted:
</p><ul>

<li>Demand Gen line items, ad groups, and the relevant targeting will be included in relevant <code>get</code> and <code>list</code> requests.
<li>Demand Gen line items, ad groups, ads, and the relevant targeting can be created, deleted, and updated.</li></ul>

<p>
We aim to launch full Demand Gen support for the Display & Video 360 API in general availability later this year. This will be announced with a future release.
</p>
<p>
For a complete list of new features in this update, see the Display & Video 360 API <a href="https://developers.google.com/display-video/api/release-notes#april_14_2026?utm_source=googleblog-adsdevs&amp;utm_medium=blog&amp;utm_campaign=adr-dv360api-update-apr2026&amp;utm_content=learnmore-refdocs" target="_blank">release notes</a>. Before using these new features, make sure to update your <a href="https://developers.google.com/display-video/api/guides/getting-started/libraries?utm_source=googleblog-adsdevs&amp;utm_medium=blog&amp;utm_campaign=adr-dv360api-update-apr2026&amp;utm_content=learnmore-refdocs" target="_blank">client library to the latest version</a>.
</p>
